How does San Jose Airport's Fire Readiness Stack Up?

The FAA does not set minimum staffing requirements for airport fire stations. The NBC Bay Area Investigative Unit compared San Jose Airport to 10 other airports of similar size. SJC has the fewest firefighters per shift of any of the airports we examined.
